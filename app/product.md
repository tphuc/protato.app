# Product Description: Protato 3D Mockup

**Protato** is a premium 3D design and visualization tool designed specifically for developers, designers, and marketers who need high-fidelity device mockups. By leveraging Apple's RealityKit and Metal, Protato provides a seamless, studio-quality experience for creating stunning visuals of your applications on the latest hardware.

---

## 🌟 Core Value Proposition
Protato eliminates the need for complex 3D software. It offers a "what you see is what you get" editor where you can drop your screenshots or screen recordings into a 3D environment, customize the lighting and materials, and export professional-grade assets in seconds.

---

## 🚀 Key Features (Shared across iOS & macOS)

### 📱 1. High-Fidelity 3D Models
Access a curated library of the latest hardware, including:
- **iPhone 17 Series**: Air, Pro Max (Orange, White, Blue).
- **iPhone 16 Series**: Plus (Black).
- **iPhone 15 Series**: Pro Max (Black, Natural, White).
*All models are precisely detailed to match physical hardware specs.*

### 🎨 2. Screen Content Customization
Bring your static mockups to life:
- **Image Support**: Import PNG, JPG, or HEIC screenshots directly.
- **Video Support**: Apply MP4 or MOV screen recordings to the device screen with perfect looping logic.
- **Live Preview**: See your content rendered with correct perspective and screen reflections in real-time.

### 🎭 3. Professional Material & Lighting Editor
Gain full control over the aesthetic:
- **Material Tuning**: Adjust Metallic, Roughness, Clearcoat, Emissive Intensity, and Specular properties to create unique finishes (e.g., matte, gloss, or futuristic chrome).
- **Dynamic Lighting**: Configure lighting rigs to highlight specific details or create dramatic shadows.
- **Presets**: Quick-toggle between professional studio setups or create your own.

### 🎬 4. Animation Timeline
Create cinematic transitions for marketing clips:
- **Keyframe Editing**: Record camera positions (Orbit/Pan) and material changes over a timeline.
- **Variable Duration**: Support for transitions up to 2 seconds or continuous playheads.
- **Media Duration Sync**: Automatically adjust the timeline to match the length of your imported video.

### 🖼️ 5. Versatile Scenery & Backgrounds
Set the stage for your product:
- **Background Types**: Choose from Solid Colors, Linear/Radial Gradients, Custom Images, or sophisticated Mesh backgrounds.
- **Transparency**: Export with transparent backgrounds (PNG) for easy integration into other designs.
- **Aspect Ratios**: Presets for Social Media (9:16), Presentation (16:9, 4:3), and Standard (1:1).

### 📤 6. High-Quality Export
Professional-grade output options:
- **Image Export**: PNG and JPG formats with adjustable scale (up to 4K resolution).
- **Video Export**: Export animations in high-fidelity HEVC or H.264 formats.
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time export progress feedback.

## 💻 Platform Specifics

### **iOS (iPhone & iPad)**
- **Mobile Workflow**: Import directly from your Photos library or Files app.
- **Touch-First Control**: Intuitively orbit and pan using multi-touch gestures.
- **On-the-go Renders**: Generate 4K marketing assets directly from your pocket.

### **macOS**
- **Desktop Precision**: NavigationSplitView layout optimized for large displays.
- **Drag & Drop**: Effortlessly drag image and video files from Finder onto the 3D model.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Power-user controls for navigating the editor and timeline.
- **Native Experience**: Fully integrated into the macOS ecosystem with specialized settings and menu bar support.
